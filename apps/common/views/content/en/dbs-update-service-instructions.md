## Email us to use the DBS Update Service

To allow the Drugs and Firearms Licensing Unit (DFLU) to access the DBS Update Service, each authorised witness must email [dflu.dom@homeoffice.gov.uk](mailto:dflu.dom@homeoffice.gov.uk) with:

- their full name
- their date of birth
- a DBS reference (the certificate number or application reference number)
- the date of issue or application date
